# Color Palettes Catalog

> **How to use:** Choose a palette matching your aesthetic direction. Copy the HSL variables into your design tokens. All palettes include both light and dark variants. HSL format enables easy manipulation — adjust lightness for hover states, saturation for muted variants.
>
> **Contrast rule:** All text-on-background combinations listed here meet WCAG AA (4.5:1) minimum. Entries marked ✦ meet AAA (7:1).

---

## Dark Palettes

### 1. Midnight Ember
Deep blue-black with warm amber accents. Luxury lounge at midnight.

```css
/* Light */
--bg: hsl(40, 20%, 96%);  --surface: hsl(40, 15%, 100%);
--text: hsl(220, 25%, 12%);  --text-muted: hsl(220, 15%, 40%);
--primary: hsl(25, 85%, 48%);  --primary-hover: hsl(25, 85%, 40%);
--accent: hsl(350, 70%, 45%);  --border: hsl(40, 15%, 85%);
/* Dark */
--bg: hsl(220, 25%, 8%);  --surface: hsl(220, 20%, 13%);
--text: hsl(40, 15%, 90%);  --text-muted: hsl(220, 10%, 55%);
--primary: hsl(25, 85%, 55%);  --primary-hover: hsl(25, 85%, 62%);
```
